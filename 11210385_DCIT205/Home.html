Welcome to the Computer Science Department

Currently, the Department offers one undergraduate degree in computer science as well as three postgraduate programs in computer science, information technology, cyber security, and digital forensics. Parallel programs are also run by the Department. Maintaining global competitiveness in today's quickly changing environment need the abilities of a well-rounded, holistic individual with technical expertise and commercial acumen to face difficult situations. The Computer Science Department is unique in its ability to build information technology capabilities. Hello and welcome to the Department.

The Department of Computer Science is under the Faculty of Applied Sciences and has been training students in Higher National Degree programmes since 2003. Currently the department runs HND in Computer Science, Degree in Information Technology, Degree in Hardware and Networking, Degree in Web Application and Database Management, and other certificate courses. The Department trains students in various fields of computing and equip them with knowledge for development and utilization of modern computing equipment thereby placing them in positions that are in high demand in society as well as research.
